Dyspnea-12 Questionnaire

About

  • The Dyspnea-12 questionnaire is a valuable tool for healthcare providers, as it helps them better understand a patient's experience with dyspnea, monitor changes in symptoms over time, and tailor treatment plans accordingly.

    It can be particularly useful in the diagnosis and management of conditions such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), asthma, heart failure, and other respiratory and cardiovascular disorders where dyspnea is a significant symptom.

Scoring

  • The Dyspnea-12 questionnaire typically uses a scoring range. The exact scoring system can vary, but it usually involves assigning points to each response (None = 0, Mild = 1, Moderate = 2, Severe = 3), with higher scores indicating more severe dyspnea.

Results

  •   0 - 10 = Mild dyspnea
  •  11- 20 = Moderate Dyspnea
  •   >= 21 = Severe Dyspnea
